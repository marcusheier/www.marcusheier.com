About Marcus

Spiritual development with clarity, alignment, and actionable steps.

My Story

A personal path shaped by intuition, curiosity, and service.

I’m originally from Sweden, and my path began through music and sports — especially martial arts, with a focus on judo — alongside a deep connection to music, playing both drums and piano, as well as composing.

Over time, these interests took me around the world, living in different countries and experiencing a wide range of perspectives on life, discipline, and personal growth.

Through these experiences, I began to explore a deeper layer of understanding — one centered around intuition, self-awareness, and how we find direction from within.

How I Work

Mediumship and coaching held in one clear space.

Mediumship

I tune into spirit to bring forward insights relevant to your current situation, life direction, and deeper patterns.

These sessions create clarity around what is present for you — both consciously and beneath the surface — giving you a clearer understanding of where you are and what is unfolding.

Coaching

We focus on applying and integrating what has emerged into your real life.

This includes developing your own intuitive abilities, working through resistance, and building trust in your decisions — so you can move forward with clarity and consistency.
