Program Overview

Eight weeks of practice, reflection, and spiritual confidence.

This program is built in two distinct phases.

In the first four sessions, we combine intuitive guidance with your own vision. I tune into spirit to bring forward relevant insights, while you clarify your goals, direction, and what you feel called to move toward. I also share techniques that have supported me in understanding my own abilities and moving through blockages.

In Phase 2, we shift into coaching. Here, the focus is on implementing what emerged in Sessions 1-4, applying the insights, strengthening your own abilities, and building trust in your decisions and direction.

The purpose of this 8-week intensive is not only to give you insight, but to help you understand how to access and receive insights yourself.
